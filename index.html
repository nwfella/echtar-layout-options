Our Evolution

From Trading Roots to Global Solutions

Founded in 1983, ECHTAR Group (EG) began as a focused trading and distribution company. Over the last four decades, we have aggressively expanded our vision.

Today, we are one of the fastest-growing regional powerhouses, transforming from a goods importer into a comprehensive Business Solutions Provider. We bridge the gap between complex engineering theory and practical reality.

"By integrating modern 'Smart' approaches—including BIM 3D Design and Digital Engineering—we offer mutual partnership commitments built on trust, governance, and unwavering HSSE standards."
